NFS Enumeration & Access
------------------------

1. Discover NFS service
   - Use nmap to scan for NFS ports

2. List available NFS shares
   - showmount -e <target_ip>

3. Mount an NFS share
   - mkdir target_nfs
   - sudo mount -t nfs <target_ip>:/ ./target_nfs/ -o nolock

4. Access files
   - cd target_nfs
   - browse the mounted share
